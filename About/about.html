About School

Forbes College

Forbes College is a premier educational institution committed to academic excellence and holistic student development. With a strong foundation in innovation and research, the college offers diverse programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary for their future careers. Forbes College fosters a dynamic learning environment that promotes leadership, integrity, and lifelong learning.

CITE Department

The College of Information Technology Education (CITE) at Forbes College is dedicated to producing highly skilled IT professionals. With a curriculum that emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical applications, CITE prepares students for careers in software development, cybersecurity, networking, and emerging technologies. The department also provides opportunities for hands-on learning through projects, internships, and research.

FYI Organization

The Forbes Young Innovators (FYI) is an organization that empowers students to explore and excel in the field of technology and innovation. Through collaborative projects, workshops, and industry partnerships, FYI nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. The organization serves as a platform for aspiring tech enthusiasts to develop real-world solutions and contribute to the advancement of the IT industry.
